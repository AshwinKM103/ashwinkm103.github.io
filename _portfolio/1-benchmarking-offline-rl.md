Benchmarked a suite of offline RL algorithms (Decision Transformer, IQL, TD3-BC, AWAC,
ReBRAC, Behavior Cloning) on MuJoCo tasks using the **Minari** dataset format, including an
ablation that replaces `nn.Linear` layers with `NdLinear`.

- **Project page:** <https://ashwinkm103.github.io/benchmarking-ndlinear-minari/>
- **Code:** <https://github.com/AshwinKM103/decision_transformer_rl_iisc_2025>

*Supervised by Prof. Chiranjib Bhattacharyya, IISc (Jan–Apr 2025).*
