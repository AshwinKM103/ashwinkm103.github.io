**LaDRA** explores multi-agent collaboration in *latent space*: agents exchange information
by transferring KV-cache state rather than passing natural-language messages. Against
text-mediated baselines it reduces generated tokens by **33%** and wall-clock time by **28%**.

*Supervised by Prof. Y. Narahari, IISc (2026).*
